**Create Aeronautics: Encased Fluid Pipes** adds envelope-encased fluid pipe blocks to Create Aeronautics. The fluid pipe equivalent of the existing envelope-encased shaft blocks.

Wrap your Create fluid pipes in any of the 16 dye-colored hot air envelopes to route fluids through your airship hull without breaking its sealed balloon volume. Right-click with a dye to recolor in place, sneak-wrench to strip the casing back to a plain pipe, and land on them for a bounce just like the envelope blocks themselves.

Features:
- 16 dye colors matching all Create Aeronautics envelope variants
- Counts as airtight balloon volume — hot air stays sealed through the pipe
- Right-click with any dye item to recolor without breaking
- Sneak + Wrench removes only the envelope casing, returning it to your inventory
- Crafted from a fluid pipe + the matching colored envelope block
- Envelope break/place/hit sounds
